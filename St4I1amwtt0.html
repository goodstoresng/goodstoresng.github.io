What foods are rich in calcium?

Main Article

What foods are rich in calcium? Calcium is important for bone health, nails, hair, and for vision. One of the main sources of calcium is calcium-rich foods - brown rice. In addition, it is rich in calcium and other minerals, such as potassium, phosphorus, calcium and iron.Here are some of the foods rich calcium.Meat, fish and vegetables.Vegetables, fruits, and vegetables are rich in calcium. Almost all vegetables are rich in vitamin D, calcium. Some foods are also rich in potassium, iron, calcium, magnesium and calcium.Broccoli.Broccoli contains calcium, which is useful for bone health, as it contains leucine, a necessary amino acid. In addition, broccoli contains vitamin K, which is useful for vision.Legumes.Legumes are known for their high calcium content. They are known to be a source of protein, especially when added to meat. Banj hot dogs also contain a lot of calcium. Beans are rich in fiber, potassium, sodium and magnesium.Magnesium is an important mineral for vision. It helps to digest food in smaller portions, especially when combined with fruits and vegetables. In addition, beans contain high-quality protein.Broccoli.Broccoli is a very good choice because it has a high concentration of such minerals as potassium, magnesium, calcium and iron. It is also rich in fiber, potassium, iron and phosphorus.
